Questions

Is it actually free?

Yes. No trials, no premium tier, no "just $4.99/month." It's free because vocabulary building shouldn't cost money. The code is open source‚Äîyou can verify this yourself.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Your progress is stored locally in your browser. No email, no password, no "verify your account" emails. Just install and start learning.

How are the words chosen?

The database includes 100+ carefully curated SAT/GRE level words‚Äîthe kind that actually appear in educated writing. Each includes etymology, synonyms, antonyms, and example sentences. You can filter by difficulty level.

What if I miss a day?

Nothing bad happens. No guilt trips, no "you broke your streak!" notifications. Life happens. Come back whenever you want. The word will be waiting.

Does it track me?

No. Zero analytics. Zero tracking. The extension doesn't even need network access‚Äîit works entirely offline. Your vocabulary journey is private.

Can I use it for SAT/GRE prep?

Absolutely. Set the difficulty to "SAT" or "GRE" and you'll get words that commonly appear on these tests. You can export your learned words as a CSV for additional study.
